About Us

Indian food, for many, is a very confusing mix of spices, but it is this magical fusion and its intoxicating aroma that makes the food so unique. The distinct complex flavors in many Indian dishes makes it one of the most sensational cuisines in the world. Every region in the country has its own specific and identifiable food. In fact, the taste of food changes every 100 kilometers across the vast country.
